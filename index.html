Oh No Waymo!

The Fun Car Spotting Game for Kids!

How to Play

Oh No Waymo! is a fun game inspired by the classic car spotting game "Punch Buggy" (or "Slug Bug"). It's a great way to keep kids entertained on long car rides, and helps them learn to identify self-driving cars!

  1. Spot it, Shout it! The first player to shout "Oh No Waymo!" when they see a self-driving car wins a point.
  2. What's a Waymo? Explain to younger players that a Waymo is a self-driving car, which might have cameras or sensors on top and may not have a driver behind the wheel. You can also point out the specific features of the car in the picture, like the cameras and lidar on top.
  3. Be Specific (Optional): For an added challenge, players can earn bonus points for correctly identifying specific features of self-driving cars, like spinning LiDAR on the roof or cameras on the sides. (LiDAR is a laser system that helps self-driving cars see their surroundings.)
  4. Double Points! Earn double points if the self-driving car has a person in the driver's seat. Maybe they're taking over control, or maybe they're just there for safety!
  5. Keep it Safe: This game is best played from a passenger seat. Remind players not to get distracted while looking for cars.
  6. Winning! Play until you get tired, reach a pre-determined point goal, or arrive at your destination. The player with the most points wins!

Bonus Rule

Want to add a bit more silly fun? Have a designated "Fake Out" car. Maybe it's a bright orange car, or a specific model. The first player to shout "Oh No Waymo!" for the Fake Out car loses a point! (Adult supervision recommended for this rule)

Remember

This is a game for fun! Be sure to keep it light and positive, and have fun spotting those self-driving cars!
